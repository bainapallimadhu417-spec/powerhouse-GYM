Common Questions

Got Questions?
We Have Answers.

Still curious? Call us at 79895 01136 — we are always happy to help.

What are the gym timings?

+

Power House Gym is open twice daily: Morning: 5:00 AM – 10:00 AM and Evening: 4:00 PM – 9:00 PM, seven days a week. We design our schedule so both early risers and working professionals can train comfortably without any compromise to their routines.

Do you provide personal training?

+

Yes! Our experienced, certified trainers offer personalised training sessions tailored to your specific goals — whether you want to lose weight, build muscle, improve endurance, or simply get healthier. Personal training is included in Standard and Premium plans and can be added to the Basic plan at an additional cost.

Do you provide nutrition guidance?

+

Absolutely. We offer dedicated Nutrition Consulting as a standalone service and as part of our Standard and Premium membership plans. Our trainers create practical, customised diet plans that complement your training program and fit your lifestyle and food preferences perfectly.

Is the gym suitable for beginners?

+

100% yes. Power House Gym welcomes members of all fitness levels. Our trainers pay special attention to beginners — teaching proper form, building a safe and progressive routine, and creating a supportive, zero-judgment environment where you can grow confidently at your own pace.

Do you have programs for youth or teenagers?

+

Yes! Our Youth Fitness Program is designed specifically for younger members. It focuses on age-appropriate training, building coordination, discipline, and confidence — all under close professional supervision to ensure safety and healthy physical development at every stage.
